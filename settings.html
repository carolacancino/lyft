Lyft Help Driving With Lyft.

How to Change Navigation Settings

The Lyft app does not have built-in turn-by-turn navigation, but it does hook up to Google Maps, Waze, and Apple Maps (if you're on an iPhone). We've found most drivers prefer Google Maps or Waze. We highly recommend using one of these apps rather than any other in-car navigation, since they use real-time traffic information to determine the best route.

Changing your settings in the app

To choose or change your navigation app, go to the 'Settings' tab and select 'Navigation.' Toggle 'Auto Navigation' on to allow the app to automatically switch between your Lyft and navigation app.
